# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of POLYROB, pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Adopted standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** as its Code of Conduct.
The full text, including enforcement guidelines, is available at:

> <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

All community members, contributors, and maintainers are expected to abide by it.

## Enforcement contact

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at
**info@theselfrule.org**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
